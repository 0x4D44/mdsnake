  grounds while it "sits on a grip cell", but the `anchor` preset is `solid` — a segment
  can never occupy a solid cell, so "on a grip cell" is literally unsatisfiable. Adopted
  the only climbable reading consistent with "climb ALONG a grip wall": a segment grips a
  grip surface **orthogonally adjacent** to it (`gripBeside`). A snake grips a wall it is
  beside, not one it stands on. All anchor oracles authored against this model. (If a
  literal occupiable non-solid grip cell was intended, drop `solid` from the preset and
  read `cellAt(seg).grip` — noted for any future revisit; the adjacent model is the
  natural one for a climbing snake.)
